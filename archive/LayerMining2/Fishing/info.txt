Entering the fishing area gives you a rod. You can upgrade you rod with enchants

Enchants:
    - Lure f/lure fc/lure
    - Multi rod f/multirod fc/multirod
        - Using the Cancel Event action, you can have a small chance
        of being able to pull multiple times.
    - Luck f/luck fc/luck
    - Deep Lure f/deeplure fc/deeplure
        - Increases the base luck level
    - Virtual Fisher f/virtualfish fc/virtualfish
        - Have a chance to spawn a virtual fishing friend that triggers
        the same code that happens when you fish
